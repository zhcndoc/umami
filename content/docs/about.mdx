## What is Umami Analytics?

Umami is an open source, privacy-focused alternative to Google Analytics.
Umami provides you with a powerful web analytics solution that does not violate the privacy of your users.
Additionally, when you self-host Umami you are in complete control of your data.

![image](/images/docs/screenshot.png)

## Key features

- **Privacy-focused** - No cookies, no personal data collection, GDPR and CCPA compliant out of the box.
- **Lightweight tracking** - A small tracking script that won't slow down your website.
- **Custom events** - Track any user action beyond pageviews with custom event data.
- **Boards** - Build custom dashboards to visualize the metrics that matter most.
- **Insights** - Built-in reports including funnels, retention, UTM tracking, goals, and more.
- **Session replay** - Record and replay user sessions to understand behavior in context.
- **Performance monitoring** - Track Core Web Vitals and other performance metrics.
- **Teams** - Share access and collaborate with your team members.
- **API** - Full API access for building custom integrations and automations.
- **Self-hosted or cloud** - Run on your own infrastructure or use [Umami Cloud](/docs/cloud).
