---
title: Compare traffic across time periods
---

Use Umami's Compare and Breakdown insights to understand how your website's traffic is trending week-over-week, month-over-month, or year-over-year.

## Compare overall metrics

The [Compare](/docs/compare) insight lets you overlay two time periods to see changes at a glance.

### Step 1: Set your date range

Select the date range you want to analyze from the date picker. For example, choose **Last 7 days** to see the current week.

### Step 2: Open the Compare insight

Navigate to your website and open the **Compare** insight. Choose a comparison period:

- **Previous period**: Compares against the same length of time immediately before your selected range. If you selected the last 7 days, this compares against the 7 days before that.
- **Previous year**: Compares against the same dates one year ago. Useful for spotting seasonal trends.

### Step 3: Read the results

The Compare view shows:

- **Metric cards** with percentage changes. Green arrows indicate growth, red arrows indicate decline.
- **Overlaid graph** with Views (purple) and Visitors (pink) from the previous period alongside current data.

## Break down trends by dimension

The [Breakdown](/docs/breakdown) insight lets you segment your data to find *what* is driving a change.

For example, if Compare shows a 30% increase in visitors, use Breakdown to find out where they came from:

1. Open the **Breakdown** insight.
2. Select **Referrer** as the field.
3. Run the insight to see which referral sources grew or shrank.

Other useful breakdowns when investigating traffic changes:

| Field | Question it answers |
| --- | --- |
| **Referrer** | Where is the traffic coming from? |
| **Country** | Which regions are growing? |
| **Browser** | Are mobile users increasing? |
| **Path** | Which pages are attracting more visitors? |
| **UTM Source** | Which campaigns are working? |

## Practical examples

**Track a product launch**: Set the date range to the launch week and compare against the previous week. Break down by Referrer to see if press coverage or social media drove the spike.

**Monitor SEO progress**: Compare the last 30 days against the previous 30 days. Break down by Path to see which pages are gaining organic traffic.

**Evaluate a marketing campaign**: Set the date range to the campaign period and compare against the same period before the campaign started. Break down by UTM Source to isolate campaign traffic.
