---
title: Analyze traffic sources with attribution
---

Understand which marketing channels, campaigns, and referrers drive the most valuable traffic to your website by combining UTM tracking, attribution, and the breakdown insight.

## Step 1: Tag your links with UTM parameters

Add UTM parameters to all links in your marketing campaigns so Umami can identify where traffic comes from. See [Measure campaigns](/docs/guides/measure-campaigns) for details.

Example tagged URLs:

```text
https://example.com/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=spring_sale
https://example.com/?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=weekly_digest
https://example.com/?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=product_launch
```

## Step 2: View UTM data

Open the [UTM](/docs/utm) insight to see a breakdown of traffic by each UTM parameter. This shows you at a glance which sources, mediums, and campaigns are driving the most views.

## Step 3: Set up attribution

The [Attribution](/docs/attribution) insight goes deeper by connecting traffic sources to specific conversion actions.

1. Open the **Attribution** insight.
2. Choose an **attribution model**:
   - **First-Click**: Credits the first touchpoint. Useful for understanding what introduced users to your site.
   - **Last-Click**: Credits the final touchpoint before conversion. Useful for understanding what closes the deal.
3. Choose the **conversion action** (e.g., Viewed page `/thank-you` or Triggered event `purchase`).
4. Run the insight.

The results show which referrers, UTM sources, and channels contributed most to conversions under your chosen model.

## Step 4: Break down by channel

Use the [Breakdown](/docs/breakdown) insight to segment conversion data further:

- Break down by **Referrer** to see which specific sites send converting traffic.
- Break down by **UTM Campaign** to compare campaign performance side by side.
- Break down by **Country** to see which markets respond best to your campaigns.

## Putting it all together

Here's a typical workflow for evaluating a marketing campaign:

1. **Tag all campaign links** with consistent UTM parameters before launch.
2. **Monitor the UTM insight** during the campaign to watch traffic flow in.
3. **Run Attribution** after the campaign to see how many conversions it drove.
4. **Compare against the previous period** to measure the campaign's incremental impact.
5. **Break down by channel** to identify which specific placements or ads performed best.

This gives you a complete picture of your marketing ROI without relying on third-party tracking pixels or cookies.
