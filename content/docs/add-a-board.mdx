## Add a board

Log into Umami and click on **Boards** in the sidebar.

![image](/images/docs/board-navbar.png)

Click on the **Add board** button in the top-right corner.

![image](/images/docs/board-add.png)

Fill out the form with a name, optional description, and board type, then click **Save**.

## Edit or delete a board

From the **Boards** screen click the **Edit** button to update a board's name or description, or click the **Delete** button to remove it.

![image](/images/docs/board-table.png)

## Share a board

From the **Boards** screen click the **Share** button to generate a public share link. Anyone with the link can view the board without logging in.

![image](/images/docs/board-edit.png)
