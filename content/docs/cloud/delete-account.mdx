---
title: Delete account
---

> **Warning**: Deleting your account is permanent. All your data, websites, teams, and analytics history will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. If you have a paid subscription, it will be cancelled. Consider [exporting your data](/docs/cloud/export-data) before proceeding.

Once logged in, click on the side nav profile button then click **Settings**.

![image](/images/cloud/navbar-settings.png)

Navigate to **Account** and click the **Delete account** button.

![image](/images/cloud/account-manage.png)

Fill out the form details and click the **Delete** button.

![image](/images/cloud/account-delete-form.png)

Your account will now be deleted. You may sign up with the same email in the future.
