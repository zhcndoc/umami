---
title: Export data
---

Umami allows you to export your data from Umami Cloud to CSV format (compressed via gzip).

The export includes all collected analytics data for the selected website, such as page views, events, sessions, and event data. Exports are processed one website at a time. If you have a large number of websites or a large amount of data to export, please contact [support@umami.is](mailto:support@umami.is).

## Exporting data from Umami Cloud

Once logged in, click on the side nav profile button then click **Settings**.

![image](/images/cloud/navbar-settings.png)

Navigate to **Data** and click on the **Export** button.

![image](/images/cloud/export-data.png)

Fill out the form details and click the **Export** button.

![image](/images/cloud/export-form.png)

When the export is complete, you will be emailed a link to download your data.

![image](/images/cloud/export-email.png)
