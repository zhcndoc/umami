---
title: White-label
---

**White Label** lets you customize the branding on your Umami share pages with your own logo, display name, and link. White Label is available on the **Business** plan.

This is useful when sharing analytics dashboards with clients or stakeholders — instead of showing Umami branding, visitors see your company name and logo. It keeps your reporting looking professional and on-brand, whether you're an agency presenting data to clients or a business sharing public-facing analytics pages.

![image](/images/cloud/white-label-details.png)

## Configure White Label

Log into Umami and click on the side nav profile button then click **Settings**.

![image](/images/cloud/navbar-settings.png)

Navigate to **White-label** and fill out the form details.

![image](/images/cloud/white-label-form.png)

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Logo | Upload an image to display on your share pages. |
| Display Name | The brand name shown on your share pages. |
| Link URL | A URL visitors are directed to when clicking your logo or display name. |

Click the **Save** button to apply your changes.
