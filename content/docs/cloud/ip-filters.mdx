---
title: IP Filters
---

Umami allows blocking specific IP addresses or ranges, so you can filter incoming traffic and prevent unwanted or malicious connections. IP Filters is available starting at the **Pro** plan.

## Add a IP filter

Log into Umami and click on the side nav dropdown then click **Settings**.

![image](/images/cloud/navbar-settings.png)

Navigate to **IP filters** and click on the **Add IP filter** button.

![image](/images/cloud/ip-filter-add.png)

Fill out the form details and click the **Save** button.

## Edit or Delete an IP filter

Navigate to **IP filters** and click on the **Edit** button or **Delete** button next to your IP filter.

![image](/images/cloud/ip-filter-table.png)
