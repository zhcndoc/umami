---
title: IP Filters
---

Umami allows blocking specific IP addresses or ranges, so you can filter incoming traffic and prevent unwanted or malicious connections. IP Filters is available starting at the **Pro** plan.

This is useful for excluding traffic from your office, development environments, or known bot networks.

## Supported formats

- **Single IP address**: `192.168.1.1`
- **CIDR range**: `192.168.1.0/24` (matches all addresses from 192.168.1.0 to 192.168.1.255)
- **IPv6 addresses**: `2001:db8::1`

## Add an IP filter

Log into Umami and click on the side nav profile button then click **Settings**.

![image](/images/cloud/navbar-settings.png)

Navigate to **IP filters** and click on the **Add IP filter** button.

![image](/images/cloud/ip-filter-add.png)

Fill out the form details and click the **Save** button. Traffic from the blocked IP addresses will be excluded from all future data collection.

## Edit or Delete an IP filter

Navigate to **IP filters** and click on the **Edit** button or **Delete** button next to your IP filter.

![image](/images/cloud/ip-filter-table.png)
