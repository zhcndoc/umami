Share URLs make it easy to share your analytics.

By default your websiteâ€™s metrics are private. When you want to share insights, you can generate a public **Share URL** for specific views.

## What You Can Share

When you create a Share URL, you can choose exactly which views are visible. This gives you full control over what data is shared publicly.

### Traffic

High-level metrics and real-time views that show who and how visitors navigate your site.

- Overview
- Events
- Sessions
- Realtime
- Compare
- Breakdown

### Behavior

Insights into how users interact with your site and move through key actions.

- Goals
- Funnels
- Journeys
- Retention

### Growth

Metrics focused on campaigns, revenue, and attribution performance.

- UTM
- Revenue
- Attribution

## Add a Share URL

Log into Umami and click on **Websites** in the sidebar.

![image](/images/docs/navbar.png)

Click on the **Edit** button for the website you want to edit.

![image](/images/docs/website-edit.png)

Navigate to the **Share URL** section and click on the **Add** button. 

![image](/images/docs/share-url.png)

Fill out the form details and click the **Save** button.

![image](/images/docs/share-url-form.png)

The application will auto-generate a **Share URL**. Click on the link to access your share page. 

![image](/images/docs/share-details.png)
