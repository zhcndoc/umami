---
title: Collect data
---

Once you've added your website. Click on the **Get tracking code** button.

![image](/images/docs/v1/get-tracking-code.png)

From the form that pops up, copy the code and insert it into the `<head>` section of your website.

![image](/images/docs/v1/tracking-code-form.png)

Then visit your website and data should immediately appear in your Umami dashboard.

### Troubleshooting

- If your site was built with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), insert the above code block using the [next/script](https://nextjs.org/docs/pages/api-reference/components/script) component.
